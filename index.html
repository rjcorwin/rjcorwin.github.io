I code for farms.

Here's a couple reasons why.

Healthy farms grow clean water

Farms are a major source of water pollution. Farms that are better managed avoid over application of fertilizers and pesticides that would otherwise end up in our water supply. Farm thats focus on regenrative agriculture retain more of the nutrients in their soil. We can grow clean water and software can help!

Farms harvest the sun

Most of our world's energy comes from one place, the sun. Farms convert the sun's energy using nutrients and water from the land into food. Since the mid 20th century, farms have become highly dependent on synthetic fertilizer that comes from burning fossil fuels. Producing this fertilizer accounts for almost 2% of all the world's energy production. Fossil fuels are a battery of sunlight, but that battery is running out and peak oil is coming. Tomorrow's farms need to take a regenerative approach to their soil to maintain and grow their nutrients. We're building Open Source software that will empower them with a decision support system to help them reach this goal!

Smaller more distributed farms empower communities

A community that can feed its members is a community that controls its own destiny. Without dependence on centralized agriculture, issues of fluctuating food prices and supply are less of an issue. Not to mention local food tastes better and has more nutrients! Software helps smaller farms manage their land, their processes, and reach their local market in way that's competitive with Big Ag.

FarmHack.org

We are a worldwide community of farmers that build and modify our own tools. We share our hacks online and at meet ups because we become better farmers when we work together.

  • Link: http://farmhack.org

Open Pipe Kit

The Open Pipe Kit (OPK) is the missing plumbing between sensors and databases that will empower thousands of data journalists, civic hackers, farmers, etc. to collect data without needing a programmer's assistance or being locked into one data platform from a proprietary turn-key solution.

  • Link: http://farmhack.net/tools/open-pipe-kit

Farm OS

farmOS is a web-based application for farm management and planning. It is built on Drupal, which makes it modular, extensible, and secure. Both Drupal and farmOS are licensed under the GNU General Public License, which means they are open source. You can host it yourself on your own web server, or you can use Farmier, a farmOS hosting service.

  • Link: http://farmos.org
