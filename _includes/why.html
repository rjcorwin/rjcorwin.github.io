I code for farms.

Here's a couple reasons why.

Farms harvest the sun

Most of our world's energy comes from one place, the sun. Farms are society's most important way of translating sunlight into productive capacity. Fossil fuels are a battery of sunlight that's running out, farms are our future.

Smaller more distributed farms empower communities

A community that can feed its members is a community that controls its own destiny. Without dependence on centralized agriculture, issues of fluctuating food prices and supply are less of an issue. And local food tastes better too!

Healthy farms make for a healthy environment

Farms are a major source of water and chemical polution. Farms that are better managed avoid over application of fertilizers and pesticides that would otherwise end up in our water supply.
